Part Three

Part Three

Building in Public

You do not need a name, a logo or a finished product to start posting. The journey to the product is the content. Here is what pre-launch building in public looks like for Eaze Studio, and what to post on each platform.

Right now · No name locked

The Idea Stage

Post about the why before the what. The frustration that started this. The decision to build something real.

  • Why I'm building a fashion brand and what's wrong with the industry
  • What "deadstock" actually means and why it matters
  • The fabric sourcing trip to Tottenham (film it)
  • Three things I wish I knew before starting a fashion brand
  • The wardrobe problem I'm trying to solve

Soon · Name and prototype forming

The Build Stage

Show the process. The prototype sessions, the material decisions, the mistakes. This is the most compelling content you can make.

  • First prototype try-on (messy is fine)
  • Why I chose this fabric over that one
  • The name reveal and the thinking behind it
  • What made-to-order actually means for the customer
  • What I am learning from building this in public

August onwards · Pre-launch

The Launch Stage

Build the waitlist. Let people feel like they are part of it before it exists. Create anticipation, not announcement.

  • First finished piece content (the September trip)
  • How to be first on the waitlist
  • Behind the scenes of the first order being made
  • The story behind piece number one
  • Community Q&A before launch

Instagram

Your home base. Reels for discovery, Stories for daily process, carousels for storytelling. Post here first, cross-post elsewhere.

TikTok

Raw, talking to camera, unfiltered. "I'm building a fashion brand" content. The algorithm will find your person if you are specific and honest.

LinkedIn

Founder storytelling. The production worker building a fashion brand. The sustainability angle. Post once a week, same content reframed for a professional reader.
