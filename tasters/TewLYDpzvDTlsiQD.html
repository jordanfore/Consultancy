02

Your Content Mission

Why you show up online

To show the world that true health is not a body. It's a life. Through honest storytelling, expert-led content, and real community, DD The PT builds trust with the people who need it most: urban youth, teachers, and those who've been told wellness isn't for them.

How you sound on social

  • →Real, not polished. Phone-filmed, honest, from the gym floor or the park or the kitchen.
  • →Expert, not preachy. You know things. Share them with calm authority, not lectures.
  • →Community-first, not broadcast. The content invites people in. Comments, Q&As, shared experiences.
  • →Culturally aware. You speak to communities that mainstream wellness ignores. That needs to come through.
03

Your Content Pillars

Your full strategy has 6 content pillars. Here are the first 3.

1

Pillar 01 · Personal Story

The Journey: From Pitch to Purpose

Semi-pro footballer. PE teacher. ACL injury. Holistic PT. This arc is the brand. Raw, chapter-by-chapter storytelling about how life, sport, injury and purpose shaped who you are and why you do what you do. Mental health destigmatisation for urban youth runs through every chapter.

2

Pillar 02 · Injury Recovery

The Comeback: ACL Recovery in Real Time

Document the ACL recovery journey: physically, mentally and nutritionally. This is live, ongoing content with a natural narrative arc. Weekly updates showing the reality of rehab. The progress, the setbacks, the mindset work. Authenticity that money can't buy.

3

Pillar 03 · Expert Content

Holistic Health Education

Value-led content drawing on your PT, nutrition and mental health qualifications. Quick tips, myth-busting, explainer videos. "The thing your PT never tells you about..." style content that positions you as a trusted expert, not just a trainer.

Pillar 04 · Social Proof

Client Stories and Transformations

Testimonials, before-and-afters, client spotlight interviews and session clips. The community attending your Virtual Wellness Sessions for four years — those stories need to be told publicly.

Pillar 05 · Community

Wellness for the Culture

Making holistic health feel relevant for urban youth and communities left out of mainstream wellness. Commentary on mental health stigma, cultural attitudes to fitness, the school wellness gap.

Pillar 06 · The Work

Behind the Sessions

Clips from actual training sessions, Virtual Wellness Classes and school/community workshops. This is the content that was missing entirely. Show the product. Film the work.
